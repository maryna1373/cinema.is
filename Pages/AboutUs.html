Our Story

Modern Cinema is a network of theaters that has been operating in America since the 1950s. Our journey began with the founding of Silver Screen, followed by the establishment of Cinema City three years later. The latest addition to our family is Cineplex, created as a nod to modern trends, and it stands as the most innovative among our theaters. Each of our cinemas is unique, interesting, and distinctive in its own way.

What We Offer

  • Classic Cinema: In addition to new releases, we often host retrospectives of iconic films so you can enjoy the masterpieces of the past.

  • Delicious Snacks and Drinks: Our cinema bar offers a wide variety of popcorn, drinks, and other treats to make your viewing even more enjoyable.
